Hello, I’m Rakibul Hasan Shovon — call me Rak. I’m an aspiring marketing strategist and business analyst who loves turning messy signals into neat narratives. My playground sits where empathy meets evidence, and storytelling meets SQL.

Rakibul literally means Watcher, so dissecting patterns is basically destiny. Whether decoding a dataset or a cultural meme, pattern recognition is my compass.

I’m fluent in more than one professional tongue: a MA in Linguistics taught me word‑power, an MBA taught decision‑power, and an ongoing MSc in Marketing (STEM) with a Business Analytics Certificate at UW‑Milwaukee lets me crunch numbers without dulling creativity.

From Strategic Affairs Officer at North South University to a Graduate Teaching Assistant at UWM, via Nielsen focus groups, I’ve helped audiences from boardrooms to classrooms translate insight into action.

Today I blend behavioral science with BI. I build models that explain consumer decisions and dashboards that reveal what words cannot. Consider this site my live portfolio—feel free to explore.
